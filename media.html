Brief Intro/Thesis

Robert Heinlein and Joe Haldeman both lived military lives.
While Heinlein was obsessed with the Cold War and nuclear warfare,
Joe Haldeman had a different view on warfare (based on the Vietnam War)
and wrote in response to Starship Troopers.
